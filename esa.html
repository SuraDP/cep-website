About Us

The Electrical Engineering Association at NKOCET is dedicated to empowering students with knowledge and skills in electrical systems, power engineering, electronics, and emerging technologies. We foster innovation, creativity, and technical excellence in the field of electrical engineering.

From traditional power systems to smart grids, renewable energy, and IoT applications, our association provides comprehensive learning opportunities. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and industrial practice through workshops, seminars, projects, and competitions.
